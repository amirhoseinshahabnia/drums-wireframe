FAQ

How exactly does Groove AI work?

Groove AI is our new intelligent drum assistant. Groove AI analyzes your song, finds the key transients, and instantly recommends a MIDI Groove that will work perfectly for your song. And the Morph knobs let you tweak your beat even more to make any pattern match the properties of your song even more precisely. Build your entire song, including intro, verses, choruses, and more, by just dragging & dropping each groove to the Build lane. When you're ready, drag it over to your Arrange window. Bam‚Äì instant drum track.

What about Mac & PC compatibility?

If you're on a Mac, our minimum requirements for SSD 5.5 is: Intel 2.2GHz Dual Core or higher (Apple Silicon support), 4 GB of RAM. If you're on PC, minimum requirements are: 2.2GHz Intel Dual Core / AMD Processor or higher, 4 GB of RAM minimum.

Do I need iLok to use SSD?

You can activate your SSD license with Machine ID using the Pace License Manager. While you are free to use a physical iLok key if you want to, you do not need an iLok dongle.

How many installations of SSD do I get with my purchase?

You get 3 iLok licenses for each purchase of SSD 5.5.

What if I have questions about how to use SSD?

SSD is easy to use, and include built in manuals to help you get started. You can also join our incredible online community at the Steven Slate Audio Dojo where tons of fellow Slate Drums owners would be happy to share their tips and tricks with you!
